Why Us?

Welcome to the Assetto Formula Racing Website! AFR is a sim-racing server that provides multiple diffrent leagues and series to make sure everyone finds what they are looking for. Every race is live-streamed to Youtube and Twitch every saturday and sunday. For more information around the schedeal make sure to join our Discord server or check out our official website by clicking the "Join Us" button. To make sure AFR stays as one of the premier leauges in sim-racing, we update and improve our racing and streams often and are open for ideas from the community! For further question, make sure to DM any of our Discord Admins or message us on Instagram!
